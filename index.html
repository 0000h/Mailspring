Built for everyone, on every platform.

N1 is compatible with hundres for email providers, including Gmail, Yahoo, iCloud, and Micrisoft Exchange. It works great on Mac, Windows, and Linux, and supports the features you expect, like keyboard shortcuts, notifications, and multi-window controls. It's time to liberate your email from a browser tab!

Designed for Extensibility.

Want to customize your experience? No problem. N1 is designed to support complex plugins to enhance your workflows. In just a few lines of code, you can easily build new composer buttons, thread actions, sidebars, and more. Features that previously required dedicated apps, like translation or CRM sync, can now be added directly to your email experience.
